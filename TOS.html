TOS

Payment

  • 40% of the commission must be provided as a down-payment to secure the commission and begin work.
  • All payments are to be sent via PayPal in USD.
  • You have a 30 day grace period to complete any payments we agree upon. No work will be started until payment is received.
  • All payments must be completed in order for the Protogen to be shipped out.
  • Payment goes by: 40% as a down-payment, 30% once work is started, 30% once completed. Payment can be flexibe if necessary.

Cancellation and refund policy

  • I have the right to cancel any commission at any time for any reason.
  • After materials have been purchased on a commission, only partial refunds will be given.
  • After substantial work has begun on a commission, no refunds will be given.

General

  • I am a newer creator, so please be patient in the commission procress.
  • Please provide reference art or sheets, although it is not required.
  • I only make Protogen heads.
  • After the commission has been confirmed, it can take around 3-6 months to complete a Protogen. This is not a guarantee.
  • Any special modifications to the commission must be explained before work is started.
  • TOS are subject to change at any time.

Shipping

  • Shipping is done through whichever shipping service is more convenient.
  • ​​Buyers pay shipping.
  • USA shipping only.
  • Faster shipping or adding insurance to the package can be done at your expense.
  • I am not liable for shipping damages or complete loss of the package.

​By commissioning Duckies Protogens you agree you have read and agreed with everything above.
